Company

CM Games is an independent game and service developer. We focus on mobile, VR, and NFT projects. Over 400 million players have installed our hit games such as Into the Radius, Nitro Nation, Drag Racing and others.

  • 400M+installs
  • 150+employees
  • 15+games
  • 300K+daily active users
  • 11+years making games
  • 5+titles in production

The team

CM Games today is a team of more than 150 professionals in everything the world.

We welcome open communication, build transparent processes and work in a flexible schedule - so each of us can decide complex tasks, to do it comfortably and meaningfully.

We pay special attention to team development and professional development. employee growth to create unique products together. BUT more we are constantly sharing new knowledge, experimenting and not afraid to make mistakes. We sincerely love our work and are always glad to professionals who are ready join our team and contribute to CM projects games.

Our home

New work-in-progress M HQ in Tallinn.Some principles that we design by:

  • open office + LOTS of secluded meeting/call/relaxation rooms of all sizes
  • conservative design broken by colorful accents
  • all areas converging towards an event space with configurable capacity
  • transparent materials where feasible
  • plenty of natural light in work areas, more intimate call/event rooms
  • basic, practical materials, no unnecessary expenses — we always prioritize talent and customer acquisition over our ego/image
  • lots of space for work, play, rest, hide & seek, …
  • large kitchen, lots of bathrooms (this matters!)
  • great coffee, within reach
